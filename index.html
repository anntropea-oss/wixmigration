Why use a birth doula?

Having continuous support from a doula during labor and birth helps women feel confident in their ability to give birth, and increases satisfaction with the overall birth experience.

Shorter Labor

Studies show that use of a doula can shorten labor by up to 25%

Reduced Cesarean Rate

Having a doula support has been shown to reduce the cesarean rate by up to 50%

Reduced Pain in Labor

Doulas provide continuous support that has been shown to reduce the need for pain medications during labor

Reduced Stress

Doulas help women and their birth partners feel supported during labor and birth.

Better Outcomes

"Published data indicate that one of the most effective tools to improve labor and delivery outcomes is the continuous presence of support personnel, such as a doula."
